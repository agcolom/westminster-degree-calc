Undergraduate degree calculator

Use our degree calculator to get a helpful estimate of the degree result you might achieve by the end of your course, based on the marks you have so far.

Please note:

  • if you're missing any marks, you can simply enter what you expect to get
  • this tool provides an estimate only and should not be taken as a definitive prediction of your final classification
  • this calculator assumes you have already passed Level 4 (Year 1) with at least 120 credits at 40% or higher
  • results from Level 4 (Year 1) don't count towards your final degree classification

For postgraduate degrees: Switch to postgraduate degree calculator.
